Health Benefits Of using Standing Desks

Burning Calories

You can burn around 100-200 calories each hour just from standing, compared to 60-130 calories while sitting. You tend to move more when you're already standing, resulting in even more calories burned.

Improves Posture

When using a standing desk, you will have better posture. When you return to a seated position, you're more likely to maintain a good posture.

Reduces Back Pain

Standing more regularly provides relief for back pain. For many people, it can even limit the onset of back pain in the first place.

Improves Overall Mood

You feel better when you move a little more, and your overall mood improves.

Increased Focus

The basic ergonomics of a standing desk mean standing up and sitting down becomes a regular process, which helps to maintain focus.

Better Circulation

The simple movement of standing up and taking a few steps gets the blood flowing more freely around our body and therefore improves circulation.

Reduced Risk of Repetitive Strain Injuries

Ergonomic desk assessments, better chairs, and sit-standing desks all help reduce the risk of repetitive strain injuries.

More Energy

Even the slightest movements during the day help to boost your energy and keep you more alert.

Improved Metabolism

Standing desks can help boost your metabolism, leading to more efficient calorie burning throughout the day.
